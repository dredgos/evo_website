Our Philosophy

Passionate about quality and customer service. If you are looking for that one off centre piece or a storage solution for your home, office, café or bar then you have come to the right place. Over 20 years’ experience in the industry working with award winning architects, and commercial and residential clients. We strive to use locally sourced and sustainable materials where possible.

We use many materials and they all come with their own characteristics particularly timber, your furniture may have characterful grain and markings along with knots and colour variation. We see this as the stamp of bespoke and unique charm that simply isn’t realised with mass produced furniture that sits within many homes, each piece telling its own story and often becoming the talking point within its setting. There will always be an element of movement within some materials as they adjust to their environment. All items are produced in a dry workshop with special care to control air moisture and top-grade finishes applied to protect them


Timber

Delivery Information

We understand that you will wish to receive your new furniture as soon as possible. Our items are truly bespoke and are produced entirely from scratch and to achieve the highest level of quality takes time and care.

  1. Delivery Information and Lead Times

    1. As our products are made to order, delivery timescales can vary. However, they usually sit within a lead time of 10 – 12 weeks for large or detailed items so please bear this in mind at the time of order. Once your items have been completed and are ready to ship this will be arranged and coordinated to suit you where possible. Delivery of bespoke items is usually made within 10 days from completion.

    2. Some smaller items are can often be produced quicker and you will be contacted to arrange delivery. Occasionally there will be items listed that are available for immediate sale online and can be dispatched within 7 days.

    3. We only deliver within mainland UK and do not currently service areas outside of mainland UK. Any overseas deliveries will require arranging yourself.

    4. Most of our deliveries are made by external haulage companies/courier so please ensure we are notified of any issues we may need to be aware of in advance, in order to avoid failed delivery or problems with delivery. Any re-deliveries will be charged for.

    5. Please consider the access to the location of your product and ensure it will fit through doors, stair wells etc. some items will be too large to deliver in one section and may require several packages. Please discuss your requirements at design stage.

    6. If you have arranged for subcontract installers, please ensure they are aware of the products and understand their requirements.

    7. Evo Furniture ltd. will not take responsibility for mismeasurement.

  3. Product Care

    1. We are extremely passionate about our furniture with all our pieces designed to be robust and long lasting. We ensure we select the right finish for each product, based on its intended environment and use.

    2. With our products being made from natural materials there can be natural movement and change over time which is simply part of the ageing process.

    3. Tips for looking after and enjoying your furniture for years to come:

      • Evo Furniture ltd. Highly recommend not to use any standard household cleaning products or polishes and we recommend only a lightly dampened cloth to remove any surface dirt or dust and buffed dry. Do not leave any residue that can mark the surfaces.

      • Direct exposure to ultraviolet rays from sunlight can cause fading and damage to furniture, please avoid placing your item where it may be subjected to this.

      • Please avoid placement of furniture in close proximity to heat sources such as fires and radiators as this can cause excess movement within materials and could result in warping and cracking.

      • Ensure you don’t place hot or wet items directly on to your furniture and use protective mats or coasters.

      • From time to time oiled and waxed timbers will require a recoat to maintain protection and the quality of the finish and this can be dependent on usage. Evo Furniture ltd are happy to discuss and supply wood care packages so please ask.
