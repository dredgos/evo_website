Services

Situated in the market town of Royal Wootton Bassett, Wiltshire, we specialise in making bespoke, contemporary industrial style furniture and homeware made from hardwoods, metal and stone. A variety of timber and metal finishes are available to suit all tastes & preferences.

Furniture

Family dining tables, coffee tables and media units. Bring some life back into your room with a bespoke solution from us.

Home Storage

Storage doesnt have to be just functional, we can create a unique piece to solve your storage problems that you wont mind having on show.

Accessories

We dont like waste, so we use our offcuts to create all sorts of beautiful smaller pieces such as chopping boards, clocks and mirrors

Our Work

At Evo Furniture, we make each item to order so there’s no reason why you can’t get your new furniture made to a size, spec and finish that suits you. Our design team can assist with design solutions so send us a sketch or related image ideas for a bespoke quote. We can also create CAD drawings if required and upon request.

Our Philosophy

Passionate about quality and customer service. If you are looking for that one off centre piece or a storage solution for your home, office, café or bar then you have come to the right place. Over 20 years’ experience in the industry working with award winning architects, and commercial and residential clients. We strive to use locally sourced and sustainable materials where possible.

We use many materials and they all come with their own characteristics particularly timber, your furniture may have characterful grain and markings along with knots and colour variation. We see this as the stamp of bespoke and unique charm that simply isn’t realised with mass produced furniture that sits within many homes, each piece telling its own story and often becoming the talking point within its setting. There will always be an element of movement within some materials as they adjust to their environment. All items are produced in a dry workshop with special care to control air moisture and top-grade finishes applied to protect them


Timber

Specialising in hardwoods such as oak, walnut, sweet chestnut, and maple to name a few. All sourced from local saw mills.

Metals

Bespoke metal fabrication for individual requirements, use off steel, wrought iron, brass and copper. Can be supplied either unfinished, lacquered, powder coated or patinated.

Natural Stone

Various stones used in our furniture production including marble, granite and slate, all sourced from local suppliers.
